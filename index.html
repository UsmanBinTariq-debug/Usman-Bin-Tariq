About Me

With 2+ years in trading automation and AI-driven development, I specialize in...

  • Founder & CEO of Innovabotics Trading Solutions
  • Expert in Python, Pine Script, and MQL5 for bot development
  • Proven track record of 25+ deployed trading systems
  • Recently built an AI-powered research paper scraper for NeurIPS, automating categorization and analysis.

My journey began as a freelance developer on Fiverr, where I created custom trading bots for clients worldwide. One of my key projects involved developing a Python-based MetaTrader 5 bot that processes 10,000+ signals daily. This experience fueled my passion for automation and led me to establish Innovabotics, a company dedicated to empowering traders with intelligent, scalable solutions.

Outside of work, I enjoy diving into the latest advancements in AI and machine learning, exploring how they can be applied to solve real-world problems. I also spend time mentoring the next generation of developers, sharing insights on trading automation and AI. When I need a break from screens, I love reading about behavioral economics and exploring how human psychology intersects with financial markets.
